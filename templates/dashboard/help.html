Help & Support

Learn how to use the FMD Detection System

Getting Started
How to use the system:
  1. Navigate to the "Upload Image" page
  2. Take a clear photo of your cattle or select from gallery
  3. Optionally add Animal ID, location, and notes
  4. Click "Upload and Analyze"
  5. View results and recommendations
Best Practices
For accurate detection:
  • Use good lighting conditions
  • Capture clear, focused images
  • Include visible symptoms (mouth, hooves)
  • Avoid blurry or dark images
  • Take multiple angles if needed
FMD Symptoms to Look For
  • Fever and loss of appetite
  • Blisters or lesions in the mouth
  • Lesions on hooves and feet
  • Excessive drooling
  • Lameness
  • Reduced milk production
Frequently Asked Questions

The system uses advanced deep learning models trained on extensive cattle disease datasets. While highly accurate, always consult a veterinary officer for final diagnosis.
